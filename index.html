BONDING PLANETS

Bridge Time Zones Cultivate Meaningful Connections with Beloved Family and Friends

A project for my favorite family and friends

Living in New York, I face hurdles in staying connected with my loved family and friends in Taiwan due to time differences and busy schedules. To bridge this gap, I'm developing a website that pairs me with them during a daily three-hour overlap. Through random pairings and suggested topics, the platform facilitates meaningful connections, fostering emotional support and strengthening bonds despite life's chaos.
